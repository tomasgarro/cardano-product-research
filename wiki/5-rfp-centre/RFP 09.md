# RFP 09 - AI Commercial Positioning

Full tender specification: [RFP-09-AI-Commercial-Positioning.md](../../rfq/RFP-09-AI-Commercial-Positioning.md)

## At a Glance

| Item | Summary |
|---|---|
| Strategic question | Can AI become a credible Cardano adoption vertical with identifiable buyers, partners, revenue pathways, and measurable activity? |
| Core decision | Whether AI should be treated as go, conditional-go, monitor-only, or no-go as a strategic vertical |
| Evidence bar | Current market scan, buyer validation, use-case screening, competitive benchmark, Cardano-side value analysis |
| Main outputs | AI positioning assessment, use-case matrix, credibility map, adoption pathway model, go/no-go recommendation |
| Budget and dates | See the Product Research Initiatives - Grants main page |

## Evidence Gap

AI is an active commercial theme across technology and blockchain markets. Cardano may have relevant properties for agent payments, auditability, provenance, privacy-adjacent infrastructure, and deterministic settlement, but it is not yet clear whether those properties translate into buyer-relevant advantage.

This RFP should test whether AI is a real Cardano adoption vertical or mainly a narrative.

## Scope

In scope:

- external AI/blockchain demand scan;
- Cardano technical-fit validation against buyer requirements;
- neutral review of current Cardano-side AI activity;
- AI use-case screening and prioritisation;
- buyer, builder, partner, and audience credibility mapping;
- use-case-specific competitive benchmark;
- delivery, revenue, transaction, and Cardano-side value pathway analysis;
- go/no-go, conditional-go, or monitor recommendation.

Out of scope:

- AI product development;
- pilot execution;
- protocol implementation;
- technical roadmap authorship;
- marketing campaign work;
- broad AI strategy unrelated to Cardano adoption.

## Methodology Bar

Applicants should use a screening-first approach and source-date external AI market claims. Strong proposals will connect buyer problems to use cases, technical requirements, delivery pathways, revenue or transaction pathways, and measurable Cardano-side value.

Current Cardano AI activity should be reviewed neutrally, with evidence limits stated.

## Expected Decision Outputs

- AI commercial positioning assessment.
- AI use-case prioritisation matrix.
- Buyer and audience credibility map.
- Competitive benchmark.
- Current activity evidence review.
- AI adoption pathway model.
- Narrative or claim stress-test findings where proposed.
- Go/no-go recommendation and action pathway.

## Evaluation Note

Strong fit means the proposal can distinguish technical possibility from buyer demand and determine whether AI deserves strategic investment, conditional monitoring, or deprioritisation.

Common weak points include generic AI hype, unverified project claims, weak competitive comparison, and recommendations without buyer, revenue, transaction, or Cardano-side value logic.
