# Cardano Product Research - M2 RFP Package

This repository contains the Milestone 2 deliverables for the Cardano Product Committee Product Research Initiatives work.

## Contents

- `rfp-centre/` - website-ready RFP centre pages for committee review, community review, and bidder orientation.
- `rfp-full-draft/RFP-01...RFP-09...md` - full tender-ready RFP specifications for the nine Product Research Initiative grants.
- `rfp-full-draft/evaluation_framework_guidance_product_committee.md` - shared evaluation framework and process.
- `rfp-full-draft/rfp_initative_list_intro_9.md` - portfolio-level introduction to the nine research initiatives.

## Current Status

The package is prepared for committee review. Final publication dates, submission channel, clarification window, contact, and award terms should be confirmed on the Product Research Initiatives - Grants page before the call opens.

## RFP Areas

1. Customer Segmentation and Profiling
2. Use-Case Landscape and Competitive Positioning
3. Stablecoin Liquidity Incentive and Activation Strategy
4. Brand Awareness and Perception
5. Government and Emerging Markets Entry Strategy
6. Enterprise and RWA Readiness Assessment
7. L2 Adoption and Interoperability Demand Study
8. Enterprise Delivery Partner Network
9. AI Commercial Positioning
