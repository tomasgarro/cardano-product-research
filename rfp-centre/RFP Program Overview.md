# RFP Program Overview

This section contains the website-ready pages for the nine Product Research Initiative RFPs. The full tender specifications remain in the RFQ package; these pages are the public reading layer for committee review, community review, and bidder orientation.

The purpose of the programme is to commission decision-oriented product research for Cardano. Each RFP is designed to close a specific evidence gap and support a concrete action, such as prioritising a customer segment, funding a liquidity intervention, selecting a regional entry play, validating an enterprise pathway, or deciding whether a narrative should become a strategic vertical.

## Award and Scope Flexibility

The CPC may award one proposal, multiple proposals, or no proposal for a given RFP. It may also request scope clarification or negotiate a revised scope before award where that improves value, feasibility, or evidence quality.

Applicants should propose the budget required to meet the evidence standard in the relevant RFP. Budget will be assessed for value for money, not lowest price alone.
