# Clarifications & FAQ

This page is the public clarification record for the nine Product Research Initiative RFPs. It should contain bidder questions, official responses, and any formal addenda.

## How Clarifications Work

1. A prospective bidder submits a question through the official channel named on the Product Research Initiatives - Grants main page.
2. The programme team consolidates duplicates and prepares a written response.
3. The question and response are published here, anonymised to bidder identity.
4. If the response changes scope, eligibility, deliverables, timing, or evaluation, the answer is marked as an addendum and linked from the affected RFP page.

There should be no private guidance on material questions. If the answer would help one bidder prepare or price a proposal, it should be visible to all bidders.

## Good Clarification Topics

- scope ambiguity in a specific RFP;
- unclear methodology expectations;
- respondent access assumptions;
- budget or timeline format;
- submission mechanics;
- data handling or publication boundaries;
- apparent conflict between an RFP page and a shared process page.

## Not for Clarification

- private negotiation of commercial terms;
- requests for confidential information about other applicants;
- requests to rewrite the RFP around one applicant's preferred method;
- questions already answered in the RFP, [[How to Apply]], or [[Submission Pack]];
- informal lobbying for award preference.

## Question Format

Each question should include:

- RFP number and title, or "programme-wide";
- relevant section or requirement;
- the ambiguity or issue;
- the requested clarification;
- any commercially sensitive wording that should be removed before publication.
